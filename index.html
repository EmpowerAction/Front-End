Since the Great Depression, the expansion of the money supply has been an accepted solution to recessionary problems in much of the world. A solution that today hits our societies in the form of inflation and lack of transparency in its mechanisms.

Societies and their economies are changing at a dizzying pace and the current complexity can no longer be approached with the eyes of the past.

Web 3.0, the growing adoption of crypto-assets and the need for greater economic participation of people in Latin America, make this the perfect time for an innovative and sophisticated solution.

We believe that decentralized finance has the power to change the world and we seek to be the bridge that connects to the real economy and real activity.
